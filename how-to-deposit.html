How to deposit

Betway’s innovative online and mobile betting platform is the perfect tool that lets sports betting fans bet on thousands of games anywhere, anytime. This means that you can skip all the queues at the betting shop and place all of your bets directly from your mobile phone. But how is this possible? In order to start betting on all the latest football action, customers will first need to learn how to deposit using Mobile Money. Betway integrates seamlessly with Mobile Money in order for customers to easily transfer their funds from their Mobile Money accounts into their Betway betting accounts. This process happens instantly and works for both deposits and withdrawals, meaning customers never have to worry about waiting for their winnings.

The process to deposit with Betway works as follows:

  1. Go to M-PESA on your phone
  2. Select Pay Bill option
  3. Enter Business no. 880185
  4. Enter Account no: Betway
  5. Enter the Amount you want to deposit (minimum Ksh10)
  6. Enter your M-PESA PIN and Send
  7. You will receive a confirmation SMS from M-PESA
  8. Your account will automatically be updated with the amount of your deposit
  9. Go ahead and place your bets

This simple process is what makes betting with Betway so easy and it’s why customers are able to bet online and via mobile in minutes. It’s also one of the key reasons why it’s so easy for customer to bet over SMS using Betway’s simple SMS betting instructions. Currently M-PESA is the only Mobile Money operator in the Kenyan market that allows this sort of functionality with Betway’s online and mobile betting platform. Betway is currently hard at work improving their Mobile Money offering and hopes to incorporate even more Mobile Money providers in the near future.
